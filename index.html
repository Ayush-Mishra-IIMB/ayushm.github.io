About Me

Prior to joining the PhD program, I worked as an Academic and Research Associate at Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad and Indian Institute of Management Bangalore. My responsibilities included supporting teaching and research activities in the Decision Sciences area, with coursework spanning Statistics, Machine Learning, Optimization, and Generative AI. My research interests focus on tensor decomposition methods, statistical learning, and the analysis of high-dimensional data, with applications in healthcare and business analytics.
